# Maincrafts-technology-task-2




**Business Sales Dashboard Analysis | Maincrafts Technology Task**

This project presents a business sales dashboard developed as part of a task provided by Maincrafts Technology. The dashboard analyzes sales and profit data to identify the highest revenue region, the most profitable category, the impact of discounts on profitability, the most valuable customer segment, and seasonal sales trends.

**Key Business Insights:**

* The South region generates the highest revenue.
* The Clothing category has the highest profit margin.
* Heavy discounts negatively impact overall profitability.
* The South region contributes the most valuable customers.
* Sales show clear seasonal fluctuations, with peaks in early and festive months.
